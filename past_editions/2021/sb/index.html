Submissions

Submissions should follow ICSE formatting guidelines:
It must conform to the IEEE Conference Proceedings Formatting Guidelines (title in 24pt font and full text in 10pt type, LaTEX users must use \documentclass[10pt,conference]{IEEEtran} without including the compsoc or compsocconf option). They should be submitted through EasyChair.
